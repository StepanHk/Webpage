A potential bestseller in the budget segment of "smart" TVs of medium diagonal. The brainchild of Xiaomi captivates 4K-resolution images on the display of 43-inch dimensions, support for HDR for the benefit of increasing the realism of color reproduction, juicy and detailed picture as a whole. For the effect of full immersion, Xiaomi Mi TV 4S 43 was equipped with a 16-watt audio system with dual Dolby Atmos + DTS decoding. The "smart" subsystem of the model uses a PatchWall substrate based on the Android TV operating system. The smart component of the TV panel is highlighted by an extensive list of compatible applications and streaming services, as well as the Google Assistant voice assistant (called from a remote control with a built-in microphone). But for digital tuners in the bins of the TV, there was no place, which is why you will need to get an external T2 receiver to broadcast terrestrial TV programs. Connecting third-party devices to the TV panel is implemented through the interface connectors USB (2 PCs.), HDMI (2 PCs.), component and composite video inputs.
